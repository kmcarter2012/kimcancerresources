Rhode Island Resources

Explore a variety of programs and services available specifically for Rhode Island residents.

Rhode Island Resource Search Portals

  • United Way 211 Rhode Island: Statewide resource hotline and website connecting residents to health and human service needs. Call 211 for assistance.
  • FindHelp.org Rhode Island: Search for free and reduced-cost services in your area across Rhode Island.
  • THE POINT: Rhode Island's Aging and Disability Resource Center. Call 401-462-4444 for information and referrals.
  • HealthyRhode Portal: Apply for healthcare, food assistance, and other benefits through this state portal.
  • Crossroads Housing Resource Center: Centralized intake for housing assistance in Rhode Island.

Rhode Island Housing Resources

  • RIHousing: Rhode Island's housing finance agency offering affordable housing programs, rental assistance, and homebuyer resources.
  • Rental Resource Guide: Comprehensive guide to rental assistance programs throughout Rhode Island.
  • Home Repair Programs: Assistance for home modifications, repairs, and weatherization.
  • FirstHomeRI: Down payment assistance and affordable mortgages for first-time homebuyers.
  • Coordinated Entry System: Single point of entry for individuals and families experiencing homelessness.
  • Crossroads Rhode Island: Housing and supportive services for individuals and families experiencing homelessness.
  • Community Action Agencies: Housing assistance through local community action programs.
  • Housing Network of Rhode Island: Coalition of non-profit housing developers providing affordable housing options.

Rhode Island Utility Assistance

  • Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP): Helps low-income households with heating costs during winter months.
  • Weatherization Assistance Program (WAP): Free weatherization services to reduce energy costs for eligible households.
  • National Grid Customer Assistance Programs: Discount rates, budget billing, and payment plans for eligible customers.
  • National Grid Special Protections: Shutoff protections for seniors, disabled individuals, and households with medical needs.
  • Community Action Agencies Utility Assistance: Emergency utility assistance through local CAP agencies.
  • Catholic Charities Emergency Utility Assistance: Help with utility bills for eligible households.
  • Salvation Army Emergency Assistance: Emergency utility assistance programs in Rhode Island.
  • United Way Utility Bill Assistance: Referrals to utility assistance programs through 211.

Rhode Island Food & Nutrition Resources

  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP): Food assistance benefits for eligible low-income individuals and families.
  • HealthyRhode - Apply for SNAP: Online portal to apply for SNAP benefits.
  • Rhode Island Community Food Bank: Network of food pantries throughout the state. Call 401-942-6325 for assistance.
  • Women, Infants, and Children (WIC): Nutrition program for pregnant women, new mothers, and young children.
  • Senior Food Box Program: Monthly food packages for low-income seniors through the Food Bank.
  • Senior Farmers Market Nutrition Program: Vouchers for seniors to purchase fresh produce at farmers markets.
  • Senior Nutrition Program: Congregate meals at senior centers and home-delivered meals for eligible seniors.
  • Meals on Wheels of Rhode Island: Home-delivered meals for homebound seniors and adults with disabilities.
  • School Meals Programs: Free and reduced-price meals for eligible children in Rhode Island schools.
  • Kids Cafe: After-school meal program for children in need.

Rhode Island Dental Resources

Explore dental care options and training-based clinics available across Rhode Island.

  • Rhode Island Department of Health Oral Health Directory: Find low-cost dental providers throughout the state.
  • Federally Qualified Health Centers: Community health centers providing dental services to underserved populations.
  • Rhode Island Donated Dental Services (DDS): Free dental care for elderly, disabled, and medically compromised individuals who cannot afford treatment.
  • Brown Dental School Clinic: Reduced-cost dental services at Rhode Island's dental teaching facility.
  • Trinity Health of New England Dental Clinics: Affordable dental care at community health centers in Rhode Island.
  • Blackstone Valley Community Health Care Dental Services: Sliding fee scale dental care for low-income patients.
  • Thundermist Health Center Dental Services: Comprehensive dental care with sliding fee discounts.
  • RIte Smiles: Dental coverage for children enrolled in Rhode Island Medicaid.

Rhode Island Senior Resources

Explore senior-focused services including benefits, caregiving support, and local aging resources.

Rhode Island Office of Healthy Aging (OHA)

  • Office of Healthy Aging: Rhode Island's state agency dedicated to serving older adults (formerly Division of Elderly Affairs).
  • OHA Get Help Portal: Central resource for all OHA programs and services.
  • OHA Contact Information: Call 401-462-3000 to reach the main office for information and assistance.
  • THE POINT: Rhode Island's Aging and Disability Resource Center. Call 401-462-4444 for personalized assistance.
  • Community Information Specialists: In-person assistance at various locations throughout the state.

Rhode Island Senior Hotlines and Helplines

  • Elder Abuse/Neglect Reporting Line: Report elder abuse 24/7 at 401-462-0555.
  • Rhode Island SHIP Medicare Counseling: Free Medicare assistance at 1-888-884-8721.
  • RIREACH Health Insurance Helpline: Call 1-855-747-3224 for health insurance questions.
  • RI Long-Term Care Ombudsman: Call 401-785-3340 for concerns about long-term care facilities.
  • United Way 2-1-1: Call 211 for 24/7 information and referrals to health and human services.
  • Behavioral Healthcare Hotline: Call 401-414-HELP (4357) for mental health crisis assistance.
  • RI Food Bank Helpline: Call 401-942-6325 for food assistance information.

Rhode Island Senior Benefits and Financial Assistance

  • Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP): Help with heating costs for eligible seniors.
  • Medicare Premium Payment Program: Assistance with Medicare premiums for eligible seniors.
  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP): Food assistance with special rules for older adults.
  • Property Tax Relief Credit: Tax credit for eligible elderly homeowners and renters.
  • Long Term Services and Supports: Financial assistance for long-term care needs.
  • Senior Legal Assistance: Free legal services for income-eligible seniors.
  • Senior Community Service Employment Program: Work opportunities for low-income older adults.
  • National Grid Discount Rates: Reduced utility rates for eligible seniors.

Rhode Island Senior Healthcare Resources

  • State Health Insurance Assistance Program (SHIP): Free, unbiased Medicare counseling.
  • HealthSource RI: Rhode Island's health insurance marketplace for those under 65.
  • Medicare-Medicaid Plan (MMP): Integrated healthcare for dual-eligible beneficiaries.
  • Rhode Island Pharmaceutical Assistance to the Elderly (RIPAE): Help with prescription drug costs.
  • Chronic Disease Self-Management Programs: Free workshops to help manage chronic conditions.
  • RI Department of Health Senior Services: Health programs specifically for older adults.
  • PACE-RI (Program of All-Inclusive Care for the Elderly): Comprehensive care to help seniors remain at home.
  • Community Health Centers: Primary care services for seniors regardless of ability to pay.

Rhode Island Senior Housing and Living Arrangements

  • RIHousing Senior Housing: Affordable housing options for Rhode Island seniors.
  • Home Repair Programs: Assistance for home modifications and repairs for older homeowners.
  • Community Development Block Grant Housing Programs: Housing assistance in Providence and other municipalities.
  • OHA Housing Resources: Information on housing options and assistance programs.
  • Rental Resource Guide: Information on affordable rental options throughout Rhode Island.
  • RentReliefRI: Rental assistance for eligible Rhode Island residents.
  • Long-Term Care Options: Information on nursing homes, assisted living, and other long-term care settings.
  • HOMES RI: Coalition addressing housing needs in Rhode Island.

Rhode Island Senior Transportation

  • RIPTA Reduced Fare Program: Free or reduced fares for seniors on Rhode Island public transit.
  • RIde Paratransit Program: ADA paratransit service for eligible seniors and people with disabilities.
  • OHA Transportation Programs: Various transportation assistance options for Rhode Island seniors.
  • Medical Transportation Management (MTM): Non-emergency medical transportation for Medicaid recipients.
  • Ancora House: Volunteer transportation service for seniors in certain communities.
  • East Bay Senior Centers Transportation: Transportation services in East Bay communities.
  • Westbay Community Action Transportation: Transportation services for seniors in West Bay communities.
  • Tri-County Community Action Transportation: Transportation for seniors in northern Rhode Island.

Rhode Island Senior Nutrition Programs

  • Senior Nutrition Program: Congregate and home-delivered meals for seniors 60+.
  • Rhode Island Food Bank: Food pantries and meal sites throughout the state.
  • SNAP Benefits for Seniors: Special application process for adults 60+.
  • Senior Food Box Program: Monthly food boxes for low-income seniors.
  • Senior Farmers Market Nutrition Program: Vouchers for fresh produce at farmers markets.
  • Meals on Wheels of Rhode Island: Home-delivered meals for homebound seniors. Call 401-351-6700.
  • CareLink Meal Services: Meal preparation and delivery for seniors with specialized diets.
  • Senior Services of Tiverton and Little Compton: Nutrition programs for seniors in these communities.

Rhode Island Senior Activities and Engagement

  • Senior Centers Directory: Find local senior centers throughout Rhode Island.
  • ServeRI Senior Volunteer Opportunities: Volunteer programs for seniors including RSVP and Foster Grandparents.
  • Age-Friendly Rhode Island: Statewide initiative to create livable communities for older adults.
  • Osher Lifelong Learning Institute (OLLI): Educational programs for adults 50+ at University of Rhode Island.
  • Providence Department of Parks Recreation Senior Programs: Activities for seniors in Providence.
  • RI Foundation Aging Initiatives: Programs promoting senior engagement and activity.
  • RI State Council on the Arts - Creative Aging: Arts programs for older adults.
  • Rhode Island PBS Adult Learning: Educational resources and programming for older adults.

Rhode Island Senior Technology Support

  • OHA Digital Equity Program: Technology training and support for older adults.
  • Cyber-Seniors Rhode Island: Free tech tutoring for seniors by trained youth mentors.
  • Senior Planet from AARP: Technology classes and resources for older adults.
  • Ocean State Libraries Computer Classes: Free technology training at libraries throughout Rhode Island.
  • Senior Community Service Employment Program (SCSEP): Includes technology training for employment.
  • Lifespan Connects: Telehealth and technology assistance for older patients.
  • Aging 2.0 Rhode Island: Innovation in aging services including technology solutions.
  • Tech4Elders: Digital literacy and device lending programs for Rhode Island seniors.

Rhode Island Senior Legal Resources

  • Rhode Island Legal Services Senior Citizen Law Project: Free legal assistance for income-eligible seniors 60+.

Providence Resources

Specific resources for residents of Providence, Rhode Island.

  • Providence Community Development Block Grant Housing Programs: Housing assistance programs within Providence.
  • Providence Department of Parks Recreation Senior Programs: Activities and programs for seniors in Providence.
