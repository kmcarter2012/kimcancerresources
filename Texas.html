Texas State Resource Guides

Comprehensive guides and directories provided by state agencies to help navigate available resources.

  • 2-1-1 Texas: Call 211 or visit the website to connect with local resources throughout Texas including housing, utilities, food, health services, and more. This statewide service helps Texas residents find local support 24/7.
  • Texas Health and Human Services Commission (HHSC): State-level guide covering public assistance programs, healthcare resources, and community resources throughout Texas.
  • Cancer Resources for Texas: Detailed guide of healthcare navigation resources, medical assistance programs, and support services for cancer patients throughout Texas.
  • Your Texas Benefits: Texas's online portal to apply for and manage benefits including healthcare, food assistance, cash assistance, and emergency relief.
  • Texas Department of Public Safety - Driver License Resources: Information on state ID assistance, driver's license resources, and other documentation support.
  • Texas Workforce Commission Vocational Rehabilitation Services: Provides specialized employment and education-related services to help individuals with disabilities achieve employment and self-sufficiency.
  • Services for People Who Are Blind or Visually Impaired: Resources for visually impaired Texas residents, including rehabilitation services, training, and technological assistance.
  • Texas HHS Office Locations: Find local HHS offices throughout Texas for in-person assistance with state programs and benefits.
  • Texas Assistance Programs: Comprehensive listing of assistance programs available to Texas residents.
  • Texas Veterans Commission: Resources and benefits for veterans in Texas. Phone: (800) 252-8387
  • Texas Law Help: Legal resources and self-help information for low-income Texans. Phone: (800) 733-8394
  • 211 Texas Resource Search: Search for food, housing, child care, crisis counseling, substance abuse treatment, and more across Texas via the 2‑1‑1 network.
  • 2‑1‑1 Texas Guided Search: Guided wizard to quickly find community assistance programs statewide, including health, housing, and financial support.
  • Texas HHS Find Services: Search by county or ZIP for HHS offices offering WIC, SNAP, TANF, Medicaid, aging, disability, behavioral health, and other services.
