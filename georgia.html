Georgia Resources

Explore a variety of programs and services available specifically for Georgia residents.

Georgia Resource Search Portals

  • 211 Georgia: The comprehensive information and referral service that connects people to resources in their community including food, housing, health services, and emergency assistance.
  • Georgia.gov Services: Official Georgia state services portal to find benefits, assistance programs, and support services statewide.
  • Georgia DFCS Services: Department of Family and Children Services portal for finding benefits and social services throughout Georgia.
  • Georgia Senior Guide: Comprehensive directory of senior housing, care services, and resources throughout Georgia.
  • Georgia Caregiver Resource Center: Statewide resource for family caregivers providing support, education, and resources.

Georgia Housing Resources

  • Georgia Department of Community Affairs: Provides information on affordable housing, homelessness prevention, and housing assistance programs for Georgia residents.
  • Georgia Housing Finance Authority: Offers resources for affordable rental housing and homeownership programs throughout Georgia.
  • DCA Housing for Special Needs: Provides various housing assistance programs for individuals with special needs and disabilities.
  • Georgia Housing Data: Comprehensive housing information and resources for renters and homebuyers.
  • Georgia Housing Search: Search tool for affordable housing, subsidized housing, and accessible units throughout Georgia.
  • Habitat for Humanity Georgia: Multiple affiliate organizations throughout Georgia providing affordable homeownership opportunities.
  • Georgia Coalition to End Homelessness: Resources for preventing and ending homelessness with housing assistance programs.
  • United Way of Greater Atlanta Housing: Housing stability programs and rental assistance for families in crisis.
  • Georgia Rental Assistance Program: Emergency rental assistance for eligible households impacted by COVID-19.

Georgia Utility Assistance

  • Georgia Energy Assistance Program: Information on Georgia programs that help with utility bills through the Department of Community Affairs.
  • LIHEAP Georgia: Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program providing financial assistance for heating and cooling bills.
  • Georgia Power Payment Assistance: Programs to help customers manage their electric bills including payment plans and energy efficiency programs.
  • Atlanta Gas Light Payment Assistance: Natural gas bill assistance programs for eligible customers.
  • Salvation Army Georgia: Emergency utility assistance programs throughout Georgia to prevent service disconnections.
  • Catholic Charities Atlanta: Emergency financial assistance including utility bill help for those in need.
  • Federal Energy Assistance: Federal resources for energy bill assistance that Georgia residents can access.
  • Georgia Power Low Income Programs: Special utility rate programs and energy efficiency assistance for qualifying low-income customers.
  • SCANA Energy Bill Assistance: Payment assistance programs and budget billing options for natural gas customers.
  • Georgia Weatherization Assistance Program: Free weatherization services to help reduce energy costs for low-income households.

Georgia Food & Nutrition Resources

  • Georgia DFCS Food Assistance: Apply for SNAP, WIC, and other food assistance programs through the Department of Family and Children Services.
  • Georgia COMPASS: Online portal to apply for SNAP (food stamps), Medicaid, and other public benefits.
  • Georgia Department of Human Services Aging Division: Senior nutrition programs including home-delivered meals and congregate dining.
  • Atlanta Community Food Bank: Provides food assistance and connects people to local food pantries throughout North Georgia.
  • Second Harvest of South Georgia: Food bank serving South Georgia communities with food pantries and mobile food programs.
  • Feeding America Georgia Food Banks: Network of food banks throughout Georgia providing emergency food assistance.
  • Georgia WIC Program: Nutrition program for pregnant women, new mothers, and children under 5 years old.
  • Georgia School Nutrition Program: Free and reduced-price meals for school-age children and summer feeding programs.
  • Georgia Food Bank Association: Network of food banks serving communities across Georgia with emergency food assistance.
  • Middle Georgia Community Food Bank: Serves 24 counties in Middle Georgia with food pantries and mobile food programs.
  • Food Bank of Northeast Georgia: Provides food assistance to 13 counties in Northeast Georgia.
  • Meals by Grace: Metro Atlanta organization providing free meals and food assistance to families in need.
  • Georgia Organics Farm to School: Programs connecting schools with local farms to provide fresh, healthy food options.
  • Georgia SNAP Outreach: Help with SNAP (food stamp) applications and understanding benefits eligibility.
  • Georgia Food Stamp Office Locations: Directory of DFCS offices where you can apply for SNAP benefits in person.
  • Meals on Wheels Georgia: Network of local programs delivering meals to homebound seniors throughout Georgia.

Georgia Dental Resources

Explore dental care options and training-based clinics available across Georgia.

  • Georgia Dental Association - Find a Dentist: Directory of dentists and clinics providing care for patients with limited financial resources.
  • Georgia Federally Qualified Health Centers: Health centers providing dental care for low-income, uninsured, and underinsured patients.
  • University of Georgia College of Dental Medicine: Dental care provided by dental students under supervision. Phone: (706) 721-2117
  • Georgia Regents University Dental Hygiene Clinic: Preventive dental care provided by dental hygiene students. Phone: (706) 721-4261
  • Clayton State University Dental Hygiene Clinic: Affordable dental hygiene services. Phone: (678) 466-4720
  • Georgia State University Dental Hygiene Clinic: Low-cost preventive dental care in Atlanta. Phone: (404) 413-1212

Georgia Senior Resources

Explore senior-focused services including benefits, caregiving support, and local aging resources.

  • Georgia Department of Human Services Division of Aging Services: Comprehensive services for seniors including case management, home care, and adult protective services.
  • Georgia Adult Protective Services: Investigates reports of abuse, neglect, and exploitation of adults 18 and older who are disabled or 65 and older.
  • Georgia Aging and Disability Services Helpline: Call 1-866-552-4464 for information about services and support for older adults and people with disabilities throughout Georgia.
