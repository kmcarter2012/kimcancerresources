Michigan State Resource Guides

Comprehensive guides and directories provided by state agencies to help navigate available resources.

  • MI 211: Call 211 or visit the website to connect with local resources throughout Michigan including housing, utilities, food, health services, and more. This statewide service helps Michigan residents find local support 24/7.
  • MDHHS Comprehensive Resource Guide: State-level guide covering public assistance programs, healthcare resources, child support services, and community resources throughout Michigan.
  • McLaren Health State & National Community Resources: Detailed guide of healthcare navigation resources, medical assistance programs, and support services for patients throughout Michigan.
  • MDHHS Adult & Children's Services: Information on various state programs including adult protective services, children's services, and family support.
  • MI Bridges: Michigan's online portal to apply for and manage benefits including healthcare, food assistance, cash assistance, and emergency relief.
  • Michigan Secretary of State - Assistance Programs: Information on state ID assistance, driver's license resources, and other documentation support.
  • Michigan Rehabilitation Services (MRS): Provides specialized employment and education-related services to help individuals with disabilities achieve employment and self-sufficiency.
  • Bureau of Services for Blind Persons: Resources for visually impaired Michigan residents, including rehabilitation services, training, and technological assistance.
  • MDHHS County Offices Directory: Find local MDHHS offices throughout Michigan for in-person assistance with state programs and benefits.
  • Michigan 2‑1‑1: Find help with food, housing, utility bills, and disaster support—call, text, or search online to connect with local programs.
  • Michigan 2‑1‑1 Database Search: Database of over 27,000 local programs—search by service and ZIP code to find community resources across Michigan.
  • MI Bridges – Help Me Find Resources: Access 30,000+ state and local resources—create a MI Bridges account to explore programs and services online.
