Louisiana Statewide Resources

Explore a variety of programs and services available specifically for Louisiana residents.

Louisiana Resource Search Portals

  • Louisiana 211 / United Way: Dial 211, text your ZIP code to 898-211, or visit their online database. Connects to health and human services, crisis support, and more.
  • Louisiana Department of Health (LDH): Official source for public health information, services, and programs.
  • Louisiana Department of Children & Family Services (DCFS): Administers programs like SNAP, FITAP, child welfare, and other support services. Customer Service: 1-888-LA-HELP-U (1-888-524-3578).

Louisiana Housing Resources

Information on housing assistance, affordable housing, and homelessness prevention programs in Louisiana.

  • Louisiana Housing Corporation (LHC): Offers programs for affordable housing development, rental assistance, and homeowner assistance. Phone: (225) 763-8700 or (888) 454-2001.
  • Louisiana Emergency Rental Assistance Program (ERAP): Provides rental and utility assistance for those impacted by COVID-19 (check for current status and eligibility).
  • Louisiana Coalition to Prevent Homelessness: Advocacy and resource information. (Note: Direct shelter directories often found via 211 or local agencies).

Louisiana Utility Assistance

Find help with energy bills, water bills, and weatherization services.

  • Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) - LHC: Administered by the Louisiana Housing Corporation and distributed by local community action agencies. Helps with heating/cooling costs.
  • Entergy Louisiana - Bill Assistance: Offers programs like The Power to Care, deferred payments, and payment extensions. Call 1-800-ENTERGY (1-800-368-3749).
  • Cleco - Customer Assistance: Programs like the Power of Sharing Fund. Call 1-800-622-6537.

Louisiana Food & Nutrition Resources

Access to food pantries, SNAP (food stamps), WIC, and other nutrition programs.

  • Louisiana SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program): Apply online via the DCFS CAFÉ portal or call 1-888-LA-HELP-U.
  • Louisiana WIC Program (Women, Infants, and Children): Nutritional support for pregnant women, new mothers, and young children. Apply through local WIC clinics.
  • Feeding Louisiana: Network of Louisiana's five regional Feeding America Food Banks. Find a local food bank or pantry.

Louisiana Healthcare & Dental Resources

Find healthcare services, Medicaid information, and dental resources.

  • Louisiana Medicaid: Apply online via the Self-Service Portal or call 1-888-342-6207 for assistance.
  • Find a Health Center (HRSA): Locate Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs) in Louisiana for affordable primary care.
  • LDH Dental Health Services: Information on dental programs and finding care.
  • Louisiana Breast & Cervical Health Program (LBCHP): Provides no-cost breast and cervical cancer screenings for eligible women. Hotline: 1-888-599-1073.

Louisiana Senior Resources

Services for older adults, including in-home care, nutrition, and benefits assistance.

  • Louisiana Governor's Office of Elderly Affairs (GOEA): State unit on aging, oversees programs and services for seniors.
  • Louisiana Area Agencies on Aging (AAA) Directory: Find your local AAA for services like meals, transportation, and in-home care. (Or search via GOEA website).

Louisiana Transportation Resources

Information on public transit, paratransit, and medical transportation.

  • Louisiana Medicaid Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT): Typically arranged through your Medicaid health plan. Contact your plan's member services or call Louisiana Medicaid Customer Service at 1-888-342-6207 for guidance.
  • Paratransit services are often managed by local/regional transit authorities. Search for "paratransit [your city/parish] Louisiana".

Cancer-Specific Support (Louisiana)

Organizations providing support and financial assistance to cancer patients in Louisiana.

  • American Cancer Society - Louisiana: Programs, services, and 24/7 helpline at 1-800-227-2345.
  • Susan G. Komen: Provides resources and support for breast cancer. Check for local Louisiana events or contacts through the main Komen website (previous affiliate structure may have changed).
  • Mary Bird Perkins Cancer Center: Multiple locations in Louisiana, offers comprehensive cancer care and support services.
  • Cancer Services of Greater Baton Rouge: Financial and emotional support for cancer patients in the Baton Rouge area.
  • Miles Perret Cancer Services (Acadiana & surrounding areas): Resource center providing support, supplies, and consultations.

New Orleans / Orleans Parish Resources

Explore comprehensive support services available to residents of New Orleans and Orleans Parish.

General Assistance

  • ViaLink/211: Dial 211 or (504) 269-2673 for resources in the Greater New Orleans area.
  • New Orleans Health Department: Information on city health initiatives and services.

Housing & Shelter

  • Housing Authority of New Orleans (HANO): Administers public housing and voucher programs.
  • UNITY of Greater New Orleans: Lead agency for the homeless continuum of care; coordinates services and shelter.
  • New Orleans Women & Children's Shelter: Provides shelter and services.

Food Assistance

  • Second Harvest Food Bank of Greater New Orleans and Acadiana: Serves Orleans Parish. Find partner pantries on their website.

Healthcare

  • University Medical Center New Orleans (UMCNO): Comprehensive hospital with cancer care services.
  • Ochsner Cancer Institute: Major cancer treatment center in New Orleans.
  • Search for FQHCs in New Orleans via HRSA Find a Health Center.
