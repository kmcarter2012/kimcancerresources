Pennsylvania Resources

Explore a variety of programs and services available specifically for Pennsylvania residents.

Pennsylvania Resource Search Portals

  • PA 211: Pennsylvania's statewide resource hotline and website connecting residents to health and human service needs. Call 211 for assistance.
  • COMPASS PA: Apply for health care, food stamps, cash assistance, and many other services through this online portal.
  • COMPASS Benefits Portal: Online application for Pennsylvanians to apply for many health and human service programs.
  • FindHelp.org Pennsylvania: Search for free and reduced-cost services in your area across Pennsylvania.
  • Pennsylvania Area Agencies on Aging: Find your local Area Agency on Aging for senior services.

Pennsylvania Housing Resources

  • Pennsylvania Housing Finance Agency (PHFA): Provides affordable housing options, homeowner resources, and rental assistance programs statewide.
  • PA DHS Housing Resources: Housing assistance information through the Department of Human Services.
  • PHFA Housing Programs for Persons with Disabilities: Information on housing resources for individuals with disabilities.
  • Pennsylvania Emergency Rental Assistance Program: Helps eligible renters affected by financial hardship pay for rent and utilities.
  • Housing Counseling Agencies: Find free housing counseling services by county.
  • Supportive Housing Programs: Assistance for vulnerable populations including those with disabilities or special needs.
  • PEAL Center Housing Resources: Housing information for families of children and adults with disabilities.

Pennsylvania Utility Assistance

  • Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP): Helps low-income households with heating bills. Apply through COMPASS or your County Assistance Office.
  • LIHEAP Crisis Program: Emergency assistance for utility shutoffs or heating system failures.
  • Pennsylvania Public Utility Commission - Customer Assistance Programs: Information on various utility assistance programs including CAP, LIURP, and CARES.
  • Dollar Energy Fund: Provides utility assistance to low-income households through the Hardship Program.
  • Electric League of Pennsylvania Assistance: Connect to energy assistance programs throughout Pennsylvania.
  • United Way of Pennsylvania Utility Help: Statewide utility assistance resources and referrals.
  • Pennsylvania Assistance Programs: Comprehensive list of assistance options for utilities and other basic needs.
  • PECO Customer Assistance Programs: Help for PECO customers who are having difficulty paying their bills.
  • PGW Customer Assistance: Payment assistance programs for Philadelphia Gas Works customers.

  • School Meals Programs: Information on school breakfast, lunch, and summer food service programs.
